-----------------------

2. Perl Modules (from CPAN)

HTTP::Lite
RPC::XML
Error
XML::LibXML
XML::LibXML::Common
Algorithm::Diff
XMLRPC::Lite
Getopt::Std
Cwd
Archive::Extract
Archive::Zip


2.TWiki - version 4.2.0 (not tested on old version of TWiki) from http://www.twiki.org
