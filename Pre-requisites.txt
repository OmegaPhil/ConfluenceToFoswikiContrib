Pre-requisites:
-----------------------
1. Perl version: 5.x (this tool has been tested on 5.8.8 version. May work with old versions of Perl 5.x.x)

2. Perl Modules (from CPAN)
   * Log::Log4perl
   * HTTP::Lite
   * RPC::XML
   * Error
   * XML::LibXML
   * XML::LibXML::Common
   * Algorithm::Diff
   * XMLRPC::Lite
   * Getopt::Std
   * Cwd
   * Archive::Extract
   * Archive::Zip

3. Foswiki 1.0.x? from http://www.foswiki.org/Download
